Hello.

Everyone can benefit from counselling at one time or another in their life. We have all had times where we have felt sad, anxious, unhappy or unsettled and are unsure who we can talk to. Talking to a professional counsellor or psychotherapist can help provide you with the time and space to look at, reflect and potentially change things that are troubling you.

My Skills.

Qualifications

In my practice I use a combination of Person Centred approaches, Cognitive Behavioural Therapy, Transtheoretical Model, Compassion Focus Therapy, and Attachment, amongst others. This combination makes me well equipped to work with a wide variety of issues that may trouble a client. I aim to develop a comfortable, trusting and friendly environment by working in a non-judgemental, understanding and empathetic manner.

Some of the things I work with:

Abuse, ADD/ADHD, Addiction(s), Adoption, Anger management, Anxiety, Bereavement, Bullying, Child-Parent conflicts, Child related issues, Cultural conflicts, Cultural issues, Depression, Disability, Dissociation, Dyslexia, Eating disorders, General counselling, Health related issues, Identity problems, Infertility, Learning difficulties, Life coaching, Loss, Men's Issues, Mental health issues, Obsessions, OCD, Personal development, Phobias, Post-traumatic stress, Pregnancy related issues, Redundancy, Relationships, Self-esteem, Self-harm, Sex related issues, Sexual identity, Sexuality, Stress, Trauma, Trichotillomania, Women's issues, Work related issues and many more.
